Cal Poly's College of Engineering is one of the premier undergraducate engineering schools in the United States. It is consistently ranked in the list of top schools by U.S. News and World Report

The Cal Poly Engineering site is based on the Mezzanine Content Management System, which is in turn based on the Django web framework. Django is a popular Python-based framework that powers such high-traffic sites such as Disqus.

Several customizations were done to the site, including:

  • A "Features" app - This app allows content editors to publish rotating front page features.
  • Tour administration - Visitors to the college may sign up for a tour and get email reminders. Tour guides are assigned an get automated reminders of their upcoming tours.
  • Event Calendar - The college can maintain a calendar of events easily.
  • News Releases w/RSS - News releases are published both as normal pages and as RSS feeds, which department sites republish.

The site is responsive and works well on mobile devices.
