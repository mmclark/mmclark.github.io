The California Planning Roundtable (CPR) is an organization of experienced planning professionals who are members of the American Planning Association (APA).

The California Planning Roundtable is built on the Django web framework and has a number of custom extensions (or apps):

  • Membership Roster - A directory of members with additional contact information available to logged-in users.
  • Projects and Publications - A catalog of work the roundtable has done.
  • Meetings - A directory of upcoming and past meetings, with associated documents and presentations.
